java1 ArrayList Exercise 1
======================
Duke collected some more clues for his case. He wants to keep them in a special folder (realized as an ArrayList) of his bookshelf.

Create a new attribute of type **ArrayList** in the class **Bookshelf**. It should contain elements of type **Clue** and have the identifier *clues*.

The constructors of class **Bookshelf** creates 3 objects of class **Clue**. Complement the Constructor, so that the created objects are saved in the attribute *clues* that you created beforehand.
