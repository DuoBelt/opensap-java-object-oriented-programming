java1 Encapsulation Exercise 3 Getter and Setter 3
======================
Duke wants to introduce further security measures. It is your turn to decide, what is helpful and reasonable.

For this, have a closer look onto the classes **Story** and **Car**.

The class **Car** has some methods and attribute already defined, however they all currently lack access modifiers.

Limit access to methods and attributes in so far, as that only the calls that are currently issued from class **Story** are supported.
