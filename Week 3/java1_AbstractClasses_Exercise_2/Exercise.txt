java1 AbstractClasses Exercise 2
======================
Duke wants to have more fun with locks.

The classes **Lock1** and **Lock2** inherit from the abstract class **AbstractLock**. 
Complete the class **AbstractLock**, so that it contains all attributes and methods, that are used within **Lock1** or **Lock2**. 

For this, closely examine the attributes and method signatures of **Lock1** and **Lock2**.

Hint: This exercise does not have a class with a main file. In order to verify your solution, please click Score.
