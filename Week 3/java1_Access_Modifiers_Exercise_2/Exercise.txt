java1 Access Modifiers Exercise 2
======================
Eric wants to steal a car... you never know when you need to make a fast getaway. 
Therefore, he tests what information he can get out of the car, before taking action.
He connects his terminal to the car he wants to test, and checks what information is available.

The class **TestCar** has 4 attributes that are restricted by different access modifiers.

Implement a public method **hackCar()** in the class **Terminal**. It should take a **TestCar** as a parameter and print all attributes of class **TestCar**, which you can access from within the class **Terminal**.
