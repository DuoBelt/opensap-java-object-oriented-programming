java1 Inheritance3
======================
Next, Duke wants to get a hybrid car with a spare battery  for enhanced reach.

Define the attribute *spareBatteryReach* of type *int* in class **HybridCar** with an initial value of <span style="color:blue">200</span>.

Then, add a method  **giveTotalBatteryReach()**  to the class **HybridCar**.

It shall call the method **getReach()** of the superclass **Car** to get the base reach of the car and store it in a local variable. 
Add the the additional reach provided by the additional battery (*spareBatteryReach*) and return the total value.

Hint: Although we taught you attributes are usually private, please do not declare *spareBatteryReach* as private this time.
