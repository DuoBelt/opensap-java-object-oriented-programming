java1 Arrays Exercise 1
======================
In this exercise, we save values in an array.
All clues, that contain Duke's newest case, shall be saved centrally. 
Therefore, they are saved within his office.

Within the class **Office**, correctly declare the attribute *clues*. It shall be an array of Strings.

Complete the method **saveClues()**, by assigning the Strings *clue1*, *clue2* and *clue3* into the array *clues*.
